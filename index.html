About Project Srijan

Project Srijan is a student-led sustainability initiative focused on addressing the growing problem of electronic waste. Founded in 2023, our mission is to create a circular economy for electronics through collection, refurbishment, and responsible recycling.

Our team of passionate students works with schools, colleges, businesses, and communities to provide accessible e-waste management solutions while raising awareness about the environmental impact of improper disposal.

Our Process

01

Collection

We organize collection drives and provide drop-off points for e-waste across partner locations.

02

Assessment

Each item is carefully assessed to determine if it can be refurbished or needs to be recycled.

03

Refurbishment

Working devices are repaired, cleaned, and prepared for donation to schools and community centers.

04

Recycling

Non-repairable items are disassembled and materials are sent to certified recycling partners.

Our Impact

Through our efforts, we're not just reducing e-waste in landfills-we're creating a positive ripple effect across communities.

Environmental

Prevented 2.5 tons of hazardous materials from entering landfills and water systems.

Educational

Donated 200+ refurbished computers to underserved schools, benefiting over 1,000 students.

Community

Engaged 500+ volunteers and conducted 25 awareness workshops reaching 3,000+ people.
